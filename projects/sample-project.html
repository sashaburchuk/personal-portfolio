Title of Project

I've attempted many things in my life and believe that I am the sum of those parts. When I was younger I travelled and lived abroad so that I could write about countries in revolution and community movements. Then I worked for the Oregon State Legislature because I believed that systematic change was necessary. Afterwards I developed an interest in technology and the psychology of advertising and became a front-end developer at a leading agency. At some point I was overcome by the urge to touch and feel the things I was building so I started working with wood.

My work focuses on supporting and nurturing quiet reflection. Creating openness through the use of space, light, and form to influence human habits and behavioral patterns.
